NuHF Claw: A Risk Constrained Cognitive Agent Framework for Human Centered Procedure Support in Digital Nuclear Control Rooms ⭐ 精选
Xingyu Xiao et al.
cs.AI

Layered Mutability: Continuity and Governance in Persistent Self-Modifying Agents ⭐ 精选

cs.AI cs.CR cs.CY cs.LG
