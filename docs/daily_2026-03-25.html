From Static Templates to Dynamic Runtime Graphs: A Survey of Workflow Optimization for LLM Agents ⭐ 精选
Ling Yue et al.
cs.AI cs.CL
该论文针对LLM-based agent系统在构建可执行workflow时面临的优化问题，旨在提升其可靠性与效率。通过引入agentic computation graphs (ACGs) 框架，将现有方法按workflow结构确定时机（静态与动态）进行分类，并沿三个维度组织优化策略。具体方法包括区分可复用的workflow template、run-specific graph与execution trace，并利用task metrics、verifier signals等反馈进行优化。最终效果是提出了一个统一的评估框架，强调结合graph-level properties与execution cost，以增强workflow的robustness和reproducibility。
Benchmarking Multi-Agent LLM Architectures for Financial Document Processing: A Comparative Study of Orchestration Patterns, Cost-Accuracy Tradeoffs and Production Scaling Strategies ⭐ 精选

cs.AI cs.CL cs.LG

cs.LG cs.AI
